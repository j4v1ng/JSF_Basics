PAGE LIFECYCLE ANNOTATIONS

In this example, you will see in the console 2 messages that are displayed
when methods annotated with page lifecycle annotations are executed.
@PostConstruct: When the bean is created
@PreDestroy: Just before it bean goes out of scope
(Example: In a Request scoped bean the method will be executed, after the server response,
but just before the bean is destroyed)
