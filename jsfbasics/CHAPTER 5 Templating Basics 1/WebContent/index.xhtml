This is the index page This little program explains basic templating with JSF
  • First you create a template(A common look and fell for all the pages in your program).
    The parts of the template that may vary(differ), should be warped inside the insert tag, and also is
    very important to give to the insert tag a name.(See MasterLayot.xhtml)


  • Then all you need to do is create each different part of the template.
    For that you will create one or many composition pages that define, the elements that should be replaced in the
    template.(This page(index.xhtml), is an example of how to do it, have a look at its source code)
    You just need to use the composition and define(With the name of the insert tag in the template) tags.
